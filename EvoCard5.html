Evolved Mortar

**Evolution Ability:** The Evolved Mortar throws a goblin barrel at a 5-tile range instead of the normal shell. It has a slightly slower hit speed but spawns a single Goblin upon impact. The Goblin then targets the nearest Crown Tower. This ability only occurs on the third attack.
