The Basics of CLASH

How to Play Clash Royale

Gameplay Overview

To play Clash Royale, you build a deck of 8 cards (troops, spells, defenses) and battle 1v1 in real-time. You use Elixir to deploy cards onto the map to attack the enemy's towers while defending your own. The primary goal is to destroy the enemy's King Tower. The match lasts 3 minutes, and the winner is the player who gets more crowns (destroying more towers) than their opponent within that time. Winning earns you trophies, allowing you to climb arenas.

Key Strategies

  • **Elixir Management:** Always try to use your Elixir efficiently and avoid being at 10 Elixir doing nothing. Conversely, try to force your opponent to overspend their Elixir.
  • **Card Synergy:** Understand how your cards work together (e.g., placing a tank like a Giant in front of a damage-dealer like a Wizard).
  • **Strategic Placement:** Deploy cards carefully. Avoid clumping troops together, as they will be easily destroyed by area-of-effect spells like Fireball or Valkyrie.
  • **Baiting and Countering:** Use cheap or less important cards to 'bait out' the opponent's strong counters, then deploy your main push after their counter card is cycling.
  • **Effective Pushes:** Focus on building counter-pushes where you defend with minimum Elixir, and the surviving troops then turn into your attack force.
