The Basics of CLASH

How to Play Clash Royale

Gameplay Overview

To play Clash Royale, you build a deck of 8 cards consisting of troops, spells, and buildings. You battle another player in real-time using Elixir to deploy cards on the battlefield. The goal is to destroy the opponent's towers while defending your own. Matches last three minutes, and the player who destroys more towers wins the match. Each tower destroyed gives you a crown, and the player with the most crowns at the end wins. Winning battles earns trophies that allow you to climb arenas, unlock new cards, and upgrade your deck.

Key Strategies

  • Elixir Management – Always spend your Elixir wisely and avoid sitting at full Elixir while doing nothing.
  • Card Synergy – Combine cards that work well together, such as tanks protecting fragile damage dealers.
  • Strategic Placement – Place troops carefully to avoid giving your opponent easy value with spells.
  • Baiting and Countering – Use weaker cards to force out your opponent’s counters before playing your main push.
  • Effective Counterpush – Defend efficiently and turn surviving troops into a strong counterattack.
